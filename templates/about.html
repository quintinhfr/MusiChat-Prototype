About

Hello! Welcome to MusiChat!

We are a social media platform for musicians of all types. Our mission is to be the answer to bland musician forums, yet still allow for the same functionality. We wanted to bring musicians a place where they can post their ideas, react to other ideas, and bond with other musicians. This site aims to have all of the features of any other social media, but be geared toward those with an interest in music.
